# Complete Codebase Audit, Bug Detection & Repair

You have full access to the entire codebase.

Your task is to perform a comprehensive audit of the application and repair all verified issues while preserving existing working functionality.

## Critical Rules

* Analyze the actual codebase before making any changes.
* Do not assume features exist.
* Audit only what is implemented in the code.
* Do not remove working functionality.
* Do not rewrite stable systems unnecessarily.
* Prefer targeted fixes over large refactors.
* Maintain backward compatibility.
* Prevent regressions.
* Ensure all fixes are production-ready.

---
